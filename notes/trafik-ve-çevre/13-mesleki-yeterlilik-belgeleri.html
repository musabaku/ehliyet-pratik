Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgeleri — Professional Competence Certificates (SRC)

Last updated: 2026-04-18
Slides covered: SLIDE 1 — Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgeleri (SRC1–SRC5) + psiko-teknik belgesi

1. Overview — Görev (Purpose)

A normal B-class ehliyet lets you drive an otomobil for personal use. But if you want to drive a vehicle commercially — i.e. carrying passengers or cargo for money — an ehliyet alone is not enough. You also need:

  1. A Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgesi (SRC = Sürücü Mesleki Yeterlilik) — there are 5 types (SRC1–SRC5) depending on what you haul and where.
  2. A Psiko-teknik belgesi (psycho-technical assessment certificate) — a lab test measuring reaction time, attention, and coordination for professional drivers.

Both are obtained after getting the ehliyet. They are separate documents, not a replacement for the license.

2. SLIDE 1 — Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgeleri (SRC1 → SRC5)

What the slide showed

A title bar "MESLEKİ YETERLİLİK BELGELERİ" with the rule:

Ticari amaçla yük ve yolcu taşımacılığı yapacak sürücüler; ehliyet aldıktan sonra ayrıca mesleki yeterlilik belgesi ve psiko-teknik belgesi alması gerekmektedir.

(Drivers who will transport goods and passengers commercially must, after obtaining the driver's license, additionally obtain the professional competence certificate and the psycho-technical certificate.)

Then a numbered list of the 5 SRC certificates:

  • SRC1 : Uluslar arası yolcu taşımacılığı (International passenger transport)
  • SRC2 : Yurt içi yolcu taşımacılığı (Domestic passenger transport)
  • SRC3 : Uluslar arası eşya – kargo taşımacılığı (International goods / cargo transport)
  • SRC4 : Yurt içi eşya – kargo taşımacılığı (Domestic goods / cargo transport)
  • SRC5 : Tehlikeli madde taşımacılığı (Dangerous goods transport — a.k.a. ADR)

Three document images at the bottom: a generic Sürücü Belgesi (ehliyet), a red T.C. Ulaştırma Bakanlığı Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgesi (Ministry of Transport), and a Psiko-Teknik Değerlendirme Belgesi.

Clean English explanation

SRC certificates are issued by the T.C. Ulaştırma Bakanlığı (Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure). They are legally required for any driver whose job is to transport people or cargo for commercial gain. The two axes that split the 5 types are:

Passenger (yolcu) Cargo (eşya) Dangerous goods
International (uluslararası) SRC1 SRC3 SRC5 covers both
Domestic (yurt içi) SRC2 SRC4 SRC5 covers both

The memory hook:
- Odd numbers = international (SRC1, SRC3, SRC5 — one leg outside Türkiye)
- Even numbers = domestic (SRC2, SRC4 — both legs inside Türkiye)
- Yolcu before eşya in the numbering (1–2 = people, 3–4 = cargo, 5 = dangerous goods as a special category)

SRC5 (dangerous goods) is the outlier — it's not split by domestic/international. Anyone carrying hazardous materials (fuel, chemicals, explosives, radioactive materials) needs SRC5 regardless of route, and this is aligned with the European ADR convention.

Critical exam fact

Ticari amaçla yük ve yolcu taşıyan sürücüler için ehliyet yeterli DEĞİLDİR — ayrıca Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgesi (SRC) ve Psiko-teknik belgesi gereklidir. SRC1 = uluslararası yolcu, SRC2 = yurt içi yolcu, SRC3 = uluslararası eşya, SRC4 = yurt içi eşya, SRC5 = tehlikeli madde.

3. Psiko-teknik Belgesi (supporting concept)

A psiko-teknik değerlendirme is a lab assessment of a commercial driver's:
- Tepki süresi — reaction time
- Dikkat — attention / concentration
- El-göz-ayak koordinasyonu — hand-eye-foot coordination
- Algı — perception (depth, distance, color)

It is valid for 5 years (and must be renewed), and is legally required alongside SRC for commercial drivers. The exam may also mention psiko-teknik as a penalty/rehabilitation requirement after serious violations (e.g., alcohol over 1.00 promil) — but that's a different use-case from the SRC-linked commercial requirement.

5. Why this matters for the exam

  1. "A commercial driver needs ___ in addition to an ehliyet?" → Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgesi + Psiko-teknik Belgesi.
  2. "Which SRC do you need to drive a domestic cargo truck?" → SRC4.
  3. "Which SRC is required for transporting hazardous materials?" → SRC5 (it doesn't split by domestic/international).
  4. "An international tour bus driver must have which SRC?" → SRC1.
  5. "An ehliyet alone is sufficient to drive a commercial passenger bus. Doğru/Yanlış?" → Yanlış (ayrıca SRC + psiko-teknik şarttır).

The exam loves to swap odd/even (domestic/international) on SRC questions — memorize the mapping cold.

6. Memorize These

Ticari amaçla yük veya yolcu taşıyacak sürücüler ehliyete ek olarak Mesleki Yeterlilik Belgesi (SRC) ve Psiko-teknik Belgesi almak zorundadır.
(Drivers who will commercially transport goods or passengers must obtain an SRC and a psycho-technical certificate in addition to their license.)

SRC1 uluslararası yolcu · SRC2 yurt içi yolcu · SRC3 uluslararası eşya · SRC4 yurt içi eşya · SRC5 tehlikeli madde.

Mnemonic: Tek sayı = yurt dışı, çift sayı = yurt içi. 1–2 insan, 3–4 yük, 5 tehlike.
