Yakıt Sistemi — Fuel System

Last updated: 2026-04-13 (first class)
Covered in class: Diesel fuel system flow, diesel "air lock" faults, causes of black exhaust smoke, gasoline vs diesel comparison

1. Overview — what a fuel system does

The yakıt sistemi (fuel system) delivers fuel from the tank to the engine cylinders in the right amount, at the right pressure, at the right time. There are two main types of fuel system on the exam:

  • Benzinli (gasoline) — uses a karbüratör (in old-style systems) and a spark plug to ignite
  • Dizel (diesel) — uses a pump + injector system and hot compressed air to ignite (no spark plug)

The exam will ask you about both types and — most importantly — how to tell them apart.

2. Diesel Fuel System — Flow (Dizel Yakıt Sisteminin Çalışması)

The component chain

YAKIT DEPOSU ────▶ YAKIT GÖSTERGESİ
    │
    ▼
BESLEME POMPASI
    │
    ▼
MAZOT FİLTRESİ
    │
    ▼
ENJEKSİYON POMPASI (Yüksek Basınç Pompası)
    │
    ▼
ENJEKTÖR  ────▶ cylinder (püskürtülür / sprayed)

What each part does

  1. Yakıt deposu — Fuel tank. Holds the mazot (diesel fuel).
  2. Yakıt göstergesi — Fuel gauge. Dashboard indicator showing tank level.
  3. Besleme pompası — Feed pump. Pulls mazot out of the tank and pushes it toward the filter.
  4. Mazot filtresi — Diesel filter. Cleans the mazot of dirt and water before it reaches the injection pump.
  5. Enjeksiyon pompası — Injection (high-pressure) pump. Builds the very high pressure needed to spray mazot into the cylinder.
  6. Enjektör — Injector. The nozzle that sprays (püskürtür) pressurized mazot into the cylinder's hot compressed air.

How diesel ignition actually works (crucial exam concept)

Unlike gasoline engines, diesel engines do not have spark plugs (buji). Instead:

  1. On the intake stroke, only air is pulled into the cylinder (no fuel yet)
  2. On the compression stroke, that air is squeezed so hard it becomes extremely hot (several hundred °C)
  3. At the top of compression, the injector sprays (püskürtür) a fine mist of mazot onto that very hot air
  4. The mazot ignites spontaneously from the heat — no spark needed

The hoca's analogy: imagine a wood stove top glowing red from the fire underneath. Drop a little kolonya (cologne) or any flammable liquid on that hot surface — it ignites instantly. That's how diesel combustion works.

3. "BUNLARI UNUTMA" — Diesel System Fault: Air Lock (Hava Yapma)

The #1 diesel system fault on the exam

Dizel sistemin en çok görülen arızası sistemin hava yapmasıdır.
(The most common fault in a diesel system is air getting into the fuel system — "air lock".)

If air enters the fuel lines, the engine will not run. The hoca shared a story: a student once let the tank run completely empty during a driving lesson, the system got air-locked, and their automatic car was out of service for two full days while they bled the system.

What causes the system to get air? (All exam-tested causes)

  1. Yakıt boruları sökülür ya da temizlenirse — if the fuel lines are removed or cleaned
  2. Yakıt filtresi sökülür ya da temizlenirse — if the fuel filter is removed or cleaned
  3. Depoda yakıt biterse — if the fuel tank runs empty

What to do about it

  • Günlük olarak depoda su boşaltılmalıdır — water should be drained from the tank daily (in cold climates or for heavy use)
  • Belirli kilometrelerde hava filtresi değiştirilmeli veya temizlenmelidir — the air filter should be replaced or cleaned at certain mileage intervals
  • Yakıt tamamen bitmeden doldurulmalıdır — never run the tank completely empty

4. Egzozdan Siyah Duman (Black Exhaust Smoke) — Causes

Classic exam question: "Egzozdan siyah duman çıkmasının sebebi aşağıdakilerden hangisidir?"
(Which of the following is a cause of black smoke from the exhaust?)

The two memorize-worthy causes

  1. Hava filtresi tıkalıdır (tıkalıysa) — the air filter is clogged
  2. Yakıt pompası ayarı bozuktur — the fuel pump adjustment is off

Both cause an improper air-to-fuel ratio → incomplete combustion → black smoke + wasted fuel + environmental pollution.

Also know

  • Dizel motorlarda kızdırma bujisi silindir kapağının üzerindedir
    (In diesel engines, the glow plug is on top of the cylinder head/cover.)
    Note: A "glow plug" (kızdırma bujisi) is NOT a spark plug — it's a small heater that pre-warms the cylinder on cold starts, so the hot-air ignition works even when the engine is cold. It's the closest thing a diesel has to a "buji" and exam questions sometimes try to trick you here.

5. Benzinli Motor vs Dizel Motor (Gasoline vs Diesel) — The Master Table

This table is the single most important content in the whole fuel-system section. Memorize it cold.

Property Benzinli Motor (Gasoline Engine) Dizel Motor (Diesel Engine)
Kullanılan yakıt (Fuel used) Benzin (gasoline / petrol) Mazot (diesel fuel)
Yakıt sistemi (Fuel system) Karbüratörlü (carburetor-based) Pompa – Enjektörlü (pump + injector system)
Ateşleme sistemi (Ignition system) Buji kıvılcımı ile ateşlemeli (ignited by spark plug) Sıcak hava ile ateşlemeli (ignited by hot compressed air — no spark plug)
Emme zamanı (Intake stroke) Benzin + hava emilir (both fuel and air are drawn in) Sadece hava emilir (only air is drawn in — fuel comes later)
Sıkıştırma zamanı (Compression stroke) Benzin + hava sıkıştırılır (fuel + air are compressed together) Sadece hava sıkıştırılır (only air is compressed — until it gets very hot)
Ateşleme zamanı (Power / combustion stroke) Karışım (yakıt + hava) buji ile ateşlenir (the fuel-air mixture is ignited by the spark plug) Yakıt (mazot) püskürtülerek sıcak hava üzerinde yanar (diesel fuel is injected onto the hot compressed air and ignites spontaneously)
Sıkıştırma oranı (Compression ratio) Daha düşük (lower) Daha yüksek (higher — this is why the air gets hot enough to ignite fuel on its own)

Key takeaways from the contrast

  • No buji in a diesel — if an exam question says "diesel engine uses a spark plug," it's wrong
  • Diesel only sucks in air on the intake stroke — fuel enters later (via the injector)
  • Diesel compression is higher — this is why the air gets hot enough to ignite the fuel spontaneously
  • Gasoline consumes more because it sucks in fuel and air like a sponge; diesel is more fuel-efficient

Hoca's quote: "Bazen benzinli araç kullanan insanlar, 'benim araba çok yakıt yakıyor, su gibi içiyor' der. Çünkü sünger gibi benzini emiyor."
(Some people driving gasoline cars say "my car drinks fuel like water." That's because it absorbs gasoline like a sponge.)

6. Memorize These (Fuel System)

Dizel sistemin en çok görülen arızası sistemin hava yapmasıdır.
(The most common diesel system fault is air entering the system.)

Egzozdan siyah duman çıkmasının sebepleri: hava filtresi tıkalıdır, yakıt pompası ayarı bozuktur.
(Causes of black exhaust smoke: clogged air filter, faulty fuel pump adjustment.)

Dizel motorda ateşleme sıcak hava ile olur, benzinli motorda buji kıvılcımı ile olur.
(Diesel ignites by hot air, gasoline ignites by spark plug.)

Dizel motorda sadece hava emilir ve sadece hava sıkıştırılır; benzinli motorda benzin ve hava birlikte emilir ve sıkıştırılır.
(In diesel, only air is drawn in and compressed; in gasoline, both fuel and air are drawn in and compressed together.)

Dizel motorlarda kızdırma bujisi silindir kapağının üzerindedir.
(In diesel engines, the glow plug sits on top of the cylinder head.)
